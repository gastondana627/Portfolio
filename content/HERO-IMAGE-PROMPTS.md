# Content Creation Portfolio - Hero Image Prompts

## Image Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio) or 1200x800px
- **Style**: Modern, professional, tech-forward
- **Color Palette**: Purple/violet (#8309D5), cyan (#09C1D5), chrome/silver gradients, deep space navy
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File**: Save as `hero-content.jpg`

---

## Prompt Options

### Option 1: Creative Workspace Fusion
```
A sleek, modern creative workspace viewed from above, featuring a high-end video camera, 
professional microphone, color-graded video timeline on a monitor, graphic design tablet 
with stylus, and scattered storyboard sketches. Dramatic purple and cyan accent lighting 
creates a futuristic atmosphere. Chrome metallic elements reflect the ambient light. 
Photorealistic, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, professional photography style.
```

### Option 2: Digital Content Ecosystem
```
Abstract visualization of content creation as a flowing network of interconnected nodes. 
Video play buttons, design tools, social media icons, and creative elements float in a 
3D space connected by glowing purple and cyan light trails. Chrome metallic spheres and 
geometric shapes add depth. Dark navy background with gradient lighting. Modern, sleek, 
tech-forward aesthetic. Digital art, octane render, 4K quality.
```

### Option 3: Cinematic Production Scene
```
Professional video production setup with cinema camera on gimbal, LED panel lights casting 
purple and cyan gels, monitor displaying color-graded footage, and motion graphics elements 
floating holographically in the air. Chrome reflective surfaces, lens flares, and bokeh 
effects. Futuristic yet professional atmosphere. Photorealistic, cinematic composition, 
dramatic lighting, high contrast.
```

### Option 4: Abstract Creative Energy
```
Dynamic abstract composition representing content creation energy. Flowing chrome liquid 
metal forms intertwining with purple and cyan light beams, creating video frame shapes, 
play buttons, and design tool silhouettes. Particles and light trails suggest motion and 
creativity. Dark space background with gradient lighting. Modern, energetic, professional. 
3D render, high detail, dramatic lighting.
```

### Option 5: Tech-Forward Studio
```
Minimalist modern content studio with floating holographic interfaces showing video 
timelines, design mockups, and social media analytics. Purple and cyan neon accents 
illuminate chrome surfaces. Sleek camera equipment and design tools arranged artistically. 
Futuristic yet approachable aesthetic. Clean, professional, tech-forward. Photorealistic 
with sci-fi elements, cinematic lighting.
```

### Option 6: Content Creation Collage
```
Artistic collage-style composition featuring layered elements: video camera lens in 
foreground, color grading wheels, typography samples, motion graphics frames, and social 
media post mockups. Chrome metallic frames separate sections. Purple to cyan gradient 
overlay with light leaks. Modern, dynamic, professional. Mixed media style, high contrast, 
vibrant colors.
```

### Option 7: Minimalist Geometric
```
Clean, minimalist geometric composition with chrome metallic shapes (spheres, cubes, 
cylinders) arranged to suggest video production and design. Purple and cyan gradient 
lighting creates depth. Subtle reflections and shadows. Professional, modern, sophisticated. 
3D render, clean aesthetic, perfect for hero section, high quality.
```

---

## Recommended Approach
1. **Best for immediate use**: Option 3 or 5 (most professional and portfolio-appropriate)
2. **Most unique**: Option 2 or 4 (abstract, memorable, tech-forward)
3. **Safest choice**: Option 1 (clearly communicates content creation)

## Alternative: Use Existing Content
If you have any of your own video production stills, behind-the-scenes photos, or design 
work screenshots, those could be composed into a hero image that's authentically yours.

## Quick Implementation
Once you have the image:
1. Save as `content/assets/images/hero-content.jpg`
2. Refresh the page - it's already configured to load this image
3. The image will appear in the hero section automatically
