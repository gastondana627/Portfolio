# LoveSetMatch Logo

Please add the LoveSetMatch logo image to this location:

**File path:** `assets/lovesetmatch-logo.png`

The image should be the colorful LoveSetMatch logo with:
- Pink "LOVE"
- Blue "SET"  
- Green "MATCH"
- Three hearts at the bottom (lightbulb, medal, tennis ball icons)

This image will be displayed in the mentorship section for the LoveSetMatch Foundation card.

**Recommended specs:**
- Format: PNG with transparent background (or white background)
- Size: 400-600px wide
- Aspect ratio: Maintain original proportions

Once you add the image file, it will automatically appear in the mentorship section.
