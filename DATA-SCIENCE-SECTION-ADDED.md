## Design Decisions

**Why a separate section instead of mixing with projects?**
- Notebooks are a different format than full applications
- Allows you to showcase both without diluting either
- Easier to update and maintain
- Visitors can choose their interest area

**Placement:**
- After main projects (establishes your full-stack work first)
- Before skills (shows practical application of your data science skills)
- Accessible via navigation menu
